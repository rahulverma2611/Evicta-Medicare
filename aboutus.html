About us


Evicta Medicare Solutions is a reputable business with a long history of producing a wide variety of food products, from breakfast to dinner. The company has strengthened its position in the market by introducing a full line of premium culinary products that meet the regular requirements of a kitchen. In parallel, ongoing efforts are undertaken to preserve customer happiness and modernise the business to meet evolving customer expectations.

From the raw materials to the finished packed product, there is a focus on sanitary quality. Each of the production facilities features a cutting-edge quality control laboratory.
